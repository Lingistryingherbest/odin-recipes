Matcha Oreo Mugcake

Description

Craving a late-nigh snacchie-snack after a full day of studying? This is a quck fix for those among us who need something sweet in the next five minutes!It's incredibly moist and not rubbery like most recipes!

Ingredients

  • 60g AP flour (or 4 large tablespoons)
  • 1tbs matcha powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbs sugar (the oreos will sweeted it enough!)
  • 90ml milk
  • 15ml vegtable oil of choice
  • 3 Oreos

Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients (i.e. flour, matcha powder, baking powder, sugar).
  2. Mix in wet ingredients (i.e. milk and oil).
  3. Add in two crushed Oreos to the batter, and gently press a complete one on top of the batter
  4. Microwave for 1.5mins to 2 mins!
  5. Pair with milk for a beautiful Sunday morning breakfast.
